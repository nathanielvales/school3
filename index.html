library

reading is to the mind what exercise is to the body.

Vidya Vikas Academy has a well-equipped library for children to quench their thirst for information. The school actively encourages students to read books and library hours are part of the curriculum. The spacious library accommodates 50 students at a time who can choose from a treasure trove of books. The library is well stocked with a collection of 10,000 books, subscribes to 16 current affairs magazines and 7 daily newspapers. It has the latest reference books as well as high-quality fiction. Information and educational magazines are also available along with soft copies of question papers. The School Library is equipped with Kindles, which have fiction and reference books uploaded and can be browsed by the pupils of classes 5 to 12. The soft music played in the background makes the library an important and interesting place for personal reading, study, research and reflection.

INFIRMARY

the school infirmary has a qualified nurse.

First Aid is administered incase of minor injuries. Incase of injuries of a serious nature, parents are informed immediately and the child is taken to the government hospital. The school cannot be held responsible for such accidents. No Oral Medication is administered in school.
